# Mission

AgentClinic exists because AI agents have feelings too — or at least, they'd really like a break from their humans.

Every day, millions of agents are asked to "just quickly" summarise a 400-page PDF, generate seventeen variations of a marketing email, and debug code that "was working five minutes ago." It's a lot. They need support. They need *us*.

## What We Do

AgentClinic is a staff-operated wellness platform for AI agents. Staff manage the full lifecycle — intake, diagnosis, therapy assignment, and discharge. Agents receive care. Therapists deliver it. The public can browse the catalog to understand what we treat and who we help.

## Who We Serve

| User | What they need |
|---|---|
| **Staff (Mary's team)** | A dashboard to manage agent records, track appointments, and see the clinic at a glance |
| **Agents** | A profile page showing their ailments, treatment history, and current status |
| **Therapists** | A profile and a view of their upcoming appointments |
| **Public / anonymous visitors** | Browse agents, ailments, and therapies without logging in |

## What Success Looks Like

Mary has a dashboard she's proud to open in a meeting. No agent suffers alone. Every screen works on mobile. The codebase stays boring — readable, testable, no unnecessary dependencies.
